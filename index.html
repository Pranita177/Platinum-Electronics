TYPES OF PCBs

Production PCBs

We have the capabilities to manufacture your production printed circuit boards at our advanced manufacturing centre.

Multilayer PCBs

We provide quality multi-layered printed circuit boards ranging from 1 to 24 layers as per our client’s requirement.

Fast and Rapid PCBs

When your company needs fast & rapid PCBs, you can count on us to deliver high quality printed circuit boards.

Double-Sided PCBs

We are engaged in providing double-sided PCBs which are the universal workhorse of the electronics industry.

Single-Sided PCBs

We are engaged in providing single-sided PCBs which are the universal workhorse of the electronics industry.

Double-Sided PTH PCBs

A double-sided PTH PCB is a printed circuit board with conductive copper layers on both sides.

ENIG Finish PCBs

Electroless Nickel Immersion Gold is a PCB surface finish that provides a durable, lead-free, and solderable surface.

ROHS PCBs

Restriction of Hazardous Substances restricts the use of certain hazardous materials in electronics.

Keypad PCBs

A Keypad PCB provides mechanical support and electrical connections for keypad buttons.

Unclad PCBs

An unclad PCB is a printed circuit board without any components or plated connections.
